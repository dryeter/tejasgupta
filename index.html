Tejas Gupta

Hi! I am Tejas Gupta. I am Y2 Undergraduate Student studying mathematics at Nanyang Technological University. I am studying mathematics because solving complex problems and learning new concepts brings me immense joy. Additionally, I firmly believe that mathematics is an indispensable asset to understanding the world around us, and I hope to use such quantitive tools to further human knowledge. Throughout my academic life, I have undertaken various research projects including various projects in cryptography and deep learning. I recently also published and presented my work with Dr Yeter in ASEE Annual Conference where we explored career decision making in Singapore. I am deeply invested in social causes and actively contribute to the community around me. I have been teaching at Singapore Boys Home for 2 years and became a center-head this year. I am also the events head for NTU-Kaleidoscope, the queer society of my university. I also seek out opportunities to learn new non-academic skills, in university I started learning electronic music, theatre and inline skating and I am currently involved with organizing Perspectives (film festival) as I believe that experiencing new things and growing is crucial for a fruitful existence. I am always looking to collaborate with other people both on academic and artistic projects.
